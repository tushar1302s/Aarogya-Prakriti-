Vata
Vata is characterized by qualities of air and space. It governs movement, including breathing, circulation, and the flow of thoughts. People with a predominance of Vata tend to be creative, enthusiastic, and lively when in balance. However, imbalances can lead to anxiety, restlessness, and digestive issues.
Pitta
Pitta is associated with the elements of fire and water. It governs metabolism, digestion, and transformation in the body and mind. Individuals with a dominant Pitta nature are often intelligent, ambitious, and have strong leadership qualities. When Pitta is out of balance, it can manifest as irritability, inflammation, and digestive problems.
Kapha
Kapha is characterized by the elements of earth and water. It governs structure, stability, and lubrication in the body. People with a Kapha constitution tend to be calm, nurturing, and grounded when balanced. However, excess Kapha can lead to lethargy, weight gain, and attachment.
