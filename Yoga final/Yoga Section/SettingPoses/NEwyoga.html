Angkor Wat

: Angkor, Cambodia
-Info = Angkor Wat, originally a Hindu temple, is one of the largest religious monuments in the world. It was built in the 12th century by the Khmer Empire.

Meenakshi Amman Temple

: Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India
-Info = The Meenakshi Amman Temple is dedicated to Goddess Parvati (Meenakshi) and Lord Shiva. It is known for its intricate architecture and vibrant sculptures.

Ranakpur Jain Temple

: Ranakpur, Rajasthan, India
-Info = While a Jain temple, Ranakpur is a notable religious site with intricate marble carvings depicting Hindu deities.

Kedarnath Temple

: Kedarnath, Uttarakhand, India
-Info = Kedarnath Temple is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is part of the Char Dham Yatra.

Badami Cave Temples

: Badami, Karnataka, India
-Info = The Badami Cave Temples are rock-cut cave temples dedicated to various deities, including Lord Shiva and Lord Vishnu

Konark Sun Temple

: Konark, Odisha, India
-Info = Dedicated to the Sun God, Surya, the Konark Sun Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its impressive architecture and intricate carvings.

Brihadeeswarar Temple

: Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, India
-Info = Also known as the Big Temple, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is known for its massive Shiva lingam.

Somnath Temple

: Prabhas Patan, Gujarat, India
-Info = One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, the Somnath Temple is an ancient and revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
